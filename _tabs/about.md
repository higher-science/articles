## 关于本站 (About This Site)

探索超越传统科学框架的认知体系。本站致力于构建更完整的科学认知框架，突破传统科学体系的表象性、孤立性、底层主观性认知世界的局限，探讨系统、整体、全面认知世界的路径。

> Exploring cognitive systems that transcend the traditional scientific framework. This site is dedicated to building a more complete framework for scientific understanding, seeking to overcome the limitations of the traditional system—its focus on phenomena, its fragmented perspective, and its underlying subjectivity in perceiving the world—by investigating systemic, holistic, and comprehensive paths to knowledge.
